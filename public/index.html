Free & Cheap Domain Options Info

Cloudflare Pages gives every site a .pages.dev subdomain for free. Here are all your real options:

.pages.dev Free & Instant

Auto-assigned by Cloudflare when you deploy. No signup, works immediately.

yoursite.pages.dev

.is-a.dev Free

Free subdomains for developers. Submit a PR to is-a-dev/register on GitHub. Approved within 24–48 h.

yoursite.is-a.dev

.js.org Free

Free subdomains for JavaScript projects. Submit a PR to js-org/js.org. Reviewed within a few days.

yourproject.js.org

.eu.org Free (slow)

Free second-level domains, but approval can take weeks to months. Run by volunteers.

yoursite.eu.org

.com / .net / .org ~$10–15/yr

Standard TLDs — not free, but very affordable. Register at Cloudflare Registrar, Namecheap, or Porkbun. Connect to Pages via a custom domain.

yoursite.com

.xyz / .site / .online from $1–3/yr

Cheap alternative TLDs. Often have first-year promos. Porkbun has great prices. Not free, but nearly so.

yoursite.xyz

Truly free top-level domains (.com, .net etc.) don't exist — those require annual payment. The free options above are all subdomains on someone else's domain. The best free + instant option is always .pages.dev which you already get automatically.
